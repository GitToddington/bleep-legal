Bleep — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 20 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Bleep Ltd ("Bleep", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use the Bleep mobile application (the "App"). Bleep is a dating service operating in the Bailiwick of Guernsey. By creating an account you agree to this Policy.

1. Who we are

Bleep is operated by Bleep Ltd, Flat 48 John Henry Court, Rue Mingot, St Martin, Guernsey, GY4 6UT. For privacy questions or requests, contact contact@bleepdate.com. We are the data controller for the personal information described here.

2. Who can use Bleep

Bleep is strictly for people aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18; if we learn an under-18 has an account, we will delete it.

3. Information we collect

You give us:

  • Account details: email address and password.
  • Profile details: first and last name, date of birth, gender, photos, and the area/location you provide.
  • Optional attributes: height, marital status, education, religion, drinking and smoking habits, diet, bio, and the kind of relationship you're looking for.
  • Dating preferences: the gender(s) and age range of people you want to meet.
  • Dates you create: date type, location/venue, time, intent, and description.
  • Messages: the content of chats with people you've matched with.
  • Safety information: blocks and reports you submit.

We collect automatically: basic technical/device information needed to run the App and keep it secure.

4. How your information is shown to others

To protect your safety: before you match, other users see only your first name and a general area/suburb — not your full name or full address. After you match, the full date details (including the full address of the agreed date) become visible to your match.

5. How we use your information

To create and manage your account; show you potential dates and matches; enable chat; keep the community safe (blocks and reports); send service communications and, in future, match/message notifications; and comply with legal obligations.

Legal bases (UK/Guernsey GDPR): performance of our contract with you, your consent (optional data), our legitimate interests (safety, preventing abuse), and legal compliance.

6. Who we share it with

Other users (as in Section 4); service providers who process data on our behalf, in particular Google Firebase (authentication, database, storage); and authorities where required by law or to protect users' safety. We do not sell your personal information.

7. Where your data is stored

Your data is stored using Google Firebase infrastructure, which may process data outside the Bailiwick of Guernsey. International transfers are protected by appropriate safeguards.

8. How long we keep it

We keep your information while your account is active. When you delete your account (Section 9), we delete your profile, photos, dates, and associated content. Some data may be retained briefly for legal, safety, or fraud-prevention reasons, or in backups overwritten on a rolling basis.

9. Your rights and choices

You can access and update your profile in the App; delete your account in the App (Settings → Delete Account); and request a copy, correction, or erasure of your data by emailing contact@bleepdate.com. You may also lodge a complaint with the Office of the Data Protection Authority, Bailiwick of Guernsey (ODPA) — odpa.gg.

10. Security

We use industry-standard measures (authenticated access, encrypted connections) to protect your data and to limit what other users can see about you. No system is perfectly secure.

11. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. We will update the "Last updated" date and, for significant changes, notify you in the App.
